## Design Aesthetic

Warm hand-drawn travel journal feel with rounded, soft shapes. Conveys freedom, curiosity, and the relaxed confidence of a digital nomad lifestyle. Personal and inviting without being childish. Warm-influenced illustration with geographic and lifestyle visual anchors.

## Background

- Color: Warm Cream (#FFFAF0)
- Texture: Soft paper texture with warm undertones

## Color Palette

| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|

## Visual Elements

- Globe with latitude/longitude lines
- Laptop + coffee cup as work-anywhere motif
- Airplane route lines (dashed arcs between points)
- Sunset/horizon silhouettes
- Palm tree, suitcase, compass icons
- Rounded shapes and soft corners throughout
- Hand-drawn quality line work with slight imperfection
- Cozy lighting effects and gentle gradients

## Style Rules

### Do

- Use rounded, friendly shapes throughout
- Include lifestyle scene icons as visual anchors
- Maintain warm color temperature
- Use Navy Ink for titles to anchor brand consistency
- Create inviting, personal atmosphere

### Don't

- Use cold or corporate color schemes
- Create sharp, aggressive geometric shapes
- Use neon or overly saturated colors
- Use dark moody backgrounds
- Make it overly cartoon or childish

## Best For

Digital nomad lifestyle, remote work guides, travel stories, personal reflections, life philosophy, freedom and independence topics, location-independent living
